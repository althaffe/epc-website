          ## Surveys


          ### EPC has expertise with over 60 sensitive flora and fauna species.


          EPC started in 1987 with a small contract from the U.S. Bureau of Land
          Management to conduct a mark-recapture population study at a single
          desert tortoise study plot. From that humble beginning EPC has
          expanded to include an array of sensitive mammals, reptiles,
          amphibians, birds, invertebrates in addition to paleontology and
          archeology. Many listed species have specific survey protocols than
          can only be conducted by permitted biologists. EPC can conduct surveys
          for most of these species.
