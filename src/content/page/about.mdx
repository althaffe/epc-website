**EnviroPlus Consulting, LLC (EPC)** has provided an ever-increasing diversity of biological, archaeological, and paleontological consulting and related services to the government and private sectors since 1987. Some of these services include:

* Preparation of biological assessments, habitat assessments, and similar documents in support of permitting.
* Special status species surveys for invertebrates, fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds and raptors, and small and large mammals, and plants according to established survey protocols.
* Biological monitoring in support of construction projects. Typical project types include transmission lines, pipelines, road and highway projects, solar and wind energy development, and commercial construction.
* Phase I archaeological surveys and records searches, Native American consultation, and paleontological records searches.
* Specialized biological research and study design.
* Ground and airborne radio tracking, helicopter surveys and aerial photography.
* Ground-up development of biological/environmental training programs and videos.

EPC has distinguished itself by maintaining outstanding relations with both regulatory agencies and industry. Regulatory agencies know us for the high quality of our field and report work, adherence to permit requirements, and transparent relations. Industry clients recognize our ability to understand and find solutions to problems, timely delivery of product, and the ability to work with field staff from diverse industries.
